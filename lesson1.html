⚡ Quick Learn

Medical coding converts medical documentation into standardized codes.

  • ICD-10 → Diagnosis
  • CPT → Procedures
  • HCPCS → Equipment

Simple idea: Coding = Medical Translation.

🔍 Deep Dive

Coders review clinical documentation including physician notes, lab results, and discharge summaries.

  1. Identify principal diagnosis
  2. Identify secondary diagnoses
  3. Determine procedures performed
  4. Select most specific codes
  5. Follow official coding guidelines

Accurate coding ensures correct reimbursement and prevents claim denials.

🏥 Hospital Workflow

  1. Patient receives treatment
  2. Doctor documents visit
  3. Coder assigns codes
  4. Claim sent to insurance
  5. Payment processed
